Campus Life

school library

Was built in 1991

Our school library is really big. It has multiple different section of each genre of books. School library is the place where people have fun and enjoy.

school auditorium

Was built in 2000

School auditorium is an area where several functions takes place. It can contain a lot of people. Our school auditorium is also our badminton court. School aduditorium even has a huge stage where people can perform.

school achievements

Inter School Debate On Olympics
Won prize on Inter School Debate on Olympics

We had a MUN(Model United Nations) on Olympics. Delecates(Participants) were given different countries and has to say small speeches on conditions and the future of Olympics in there country.Our school won the best Delecate award.

Inter School Badminton Competition
Came third in the Inter School Badminton Competition.

Different age groups were representing the school, most of them got a participation certificate but U/12 division got a bronze medal.

Inter School Science Quiz and Debate
Won third prize in Inter school Science Debate and 5th in Quiz.

Grade 7 were taking part in the quiz and grade 8 in the debate. Both grade 7 and 8 had 1 team with 2 people. Grade 8th had to work in a team while Grade 7th had to alone. Both participants in 8th grade got 3rd place and 1 participant in grade 7 got 5th place.

What's New

  • New School Cafe

    Our cafe's name is Tobasco Cafe. It is a pretty big cafe. Both day scholar and boarders are allowed but its mainly for boarder's evening snacks. Day scholar can buy food from the cafe during or after school and boarders have a specific time to buy from the cafe which is after school. There are 2 options of food from the cafe , One can either take free food cooked by school or buy ready-made food. Every day there is also a special item which a student can buy like dosa , pizza , cup noodles , etc.

  • Online Attendance Management

    During online school the teacher takes the Attendance 5 mins after everyone joins in. She calls out everyone's name and marks them on the Managebac(School Website for assignments,etc.) There are 4 options P(present) , A(absent), L(Late) and H(Health).
